Module 1

Principles of process management

  • Process - all the actions, both value creating and non-value creating required to bring a product from concept to launch and from order to cash collection. These include actions to process information from the customer and actions to transform the product on its way to the customer.

    • Effective management has THREE strands

  • Quality from the point of the view of the customer
  • Understanding the process.
  • Working as one team for improvement.

    • Quality from the point of the view of the customer

  • This is the "Voice of the Customer"
  • Understand your major customers and categories of customers
  • What characteristics are most important to various customers and how does each step in the process add value for the end user?
  • Understand the value chain that creates and delivers products and services.
  • Understand how effectively your organisation works as a system, and how employees fit in (culture)
